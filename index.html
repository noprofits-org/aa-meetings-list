Why This Site Exists

When you ask AI chatbots about AA meetings, they often can't help because most meeting websites load their data using JavaScript after the page renders. This makes the meeting information invisible to AI assistants and search engines.

Traditional AA meeting websites are excellent resources for humans, but their JavaScript-loaded content can't be read by AI systems or web crawlers.

Our Solution

This directory solves that problem by generating static HTML pages with all meeting information embedded directly in the page content. Now AI assistants can read, search, and help people find AA meetings based on their specific needs.
